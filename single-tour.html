Dusty Trails Outback Adventure

Longreach - View

Venture beyond the horizon to the heart of the rugged Queensland outback. Travelling across the Great Dividing Range we pass through an everchanging land-scape before reaching our final destination in the tropical north. Experience eleven unforgettable days exploring the history, heritage and country on the quintessential Aussie Outback road trip of a lifetime.

HIGHLIGHTS

  • Meet real cattlemen during your tour of the bustling Roma Saleyards Experience the wonder of the outback night sky while star gazing in Charleville
  • Uncover the colonial heritage of outback towns at the Miles Historical Museum Village
  • Relax in the serenity on a sunset cruise on the Thomson River
  • Explore the famous Australian Stockman's Hall of Fame and Qantas Founders Museum
  • Discover the prehistoric artefacts at the Age of Dinosaurs & Finders Discovery Centre
  • Enjoy a half day exploring Magnetic Island and its surrounding turquoise waters
